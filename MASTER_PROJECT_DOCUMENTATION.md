# Sentient: Neural Social Ecosystem
## High-Fidelity Project Documentation

Sentient is a futuristic, AI-driven social media platform designed for deep cognitive synchronization and high-performance networking. This document outlines the current state of the platform, its architecture, and its primary features.

---

### 🚀 Core Features (v1.0 - Full Functionality)

#### 1. Neural Feed & Discovery
- **Smart Feed Ranking**: Posts are ranked using a multi-factor algorithm (Interests -> Mood Matching -> Engagement -> Recency).
- **Mood Synchronization**: Users can toggle between moods (Productive, Motivational, Calm, etc.) to filter their entire experience.
- **Global Discovery Fallback**: New users are automatically presented with trending global content until their personal network grows.

#### 2. Cinematic Reels System
- **Infinite Vertical Stream**: High-performance vertical video player with snap-scrolling.
- **Mood-Categorized Content**: Access to over 110+ seeded reels mapped to specific cognitive states.
- **Interactive Layers**: Real-time likes, AI-moderated comments, and share functionality.

#### 3. Ephemeral Stories (Neural Fragments)
- **Grouped Channels**: Stories are automatically grouped by user for intuitive browsing.
- **Auto-Advance Logic**: High-fidelity story viewer with progress indicators and automatic transition to the next fragment.
- **Live Ring Indicators**: Dynamic gradient rings indicate active neural channels on the home feed.

#### 5. Wellness & Cognitive Health
- **Neural Overload Protection**: Real-time tracking of session length and reel consumption.
- **Burnout Index**: Dynamic calculation of cognitive load based on interaction density.
- **Auto-Lock Mechanism**: Safety protocol that pauses access to the collective stream when critical burnout levels are detected.

---

### 💾 Mega Seeding Protocol
The platform includes a comprehensive `megaSeed.js` script designed for instant deployment of a demo-ready ecosystem.
- **Automatic Cleanup**: Wipes all non-admin collections before population.
- **Realistic Persona Generation**: 55+ users with unique skills, interests, and AI-generated bios.
- **High-Bandwidth Assets**: Integrated with Vimeo/Pexels for ultra-reliable video streams and Unsplash for high-fidelity avatars.
- **Relational Integrity**: Automatically populates likes, comments, and follower counts to simulate a mature network.

---

### 🛠️ Technical Architecture

#### Backend (Node.js & Express)
- **Scalable Controllers**: Modular logic for Reels, Stories, Posts, and User management.
- **AI Service Layer**: Integrated mood analysis and toxicity detection for comments and posts.
- **Recommendation Engine**: Custom MongoDB aggregation pipeline for interest-based ranking.
- **Data Guarding**: Robust fallbacks ensure a populated UI even with empty user follow lists.

#### Frontend (React & Framer Motion)
- **Glassmorphic Design System**: Premium "Silicon Valley" UI components with high-fidelity blur and gradient effects.
- **State Management**: Context-driven auth, mood, and wellness tracking.
- **Axios Integration**: Global interceptors with advanced error logging and session validation.
