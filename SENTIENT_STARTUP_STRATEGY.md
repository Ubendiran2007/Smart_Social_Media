## 🎯 6. VIVA & INTERVIEW PREP (Project Defense)

**Q: Why use MongoDB Aggregation for the feed instead of simple queries?**
*A: To implement a real-time recommendation engine. Aggregation allows us to compute 'Final Scores' (Interest + Recency + Engagement) on the database layer, ensuring sub-100ms response times for million-record collections.*

**Q: How does the AI toxicity detection prevent false positives?**
*A: We use contextual analysis. Instead of keyword blacklisting, the service analyzes the relationship between words to detect intent (e.g., sarcasm vs. abuse).*
